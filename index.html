Welcome to Transparent World Printing

Transparent World Printing is a Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) dedicated to helping local people in Papua New Guinea with professional printing and business support services.


Simply send us your documents for printing, photocopying, binding, scanning, and many other office support services.

About Us


Transparent World Printing is committed to providing affordable, reliable, and professional printing and office support services throughout Papua New Guinea. We support students, businesses, organizations, and individuals by delivering quality work and customer-focused solutions.


Our goal is to empower local communities by providing accessible printing, documentation, business development, and technology services.
