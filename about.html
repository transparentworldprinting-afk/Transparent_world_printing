About Transparent World Printing

Our Story

Transparent World Printing was established with a vision of providing professional printing, business support, and digital solutions to individuals, businesses, government agencies, NGOs, churches, educational institutions, and community organizations throughout Papua New Guinea.


What began as a small business support initiative dedicated to helping clients communicate, present, and manage information professionally. We believe every organization deserves access to quality services that help them grow, compete, and succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, affordable, and professional printing, documentation, ICT, and business support services that empower individuals and organizations throughout Papua New Guinea.

Our Vision

To become one of Papua New Guinea's most trusted providers of printing, business development, and digital support services, connecting communities through innovation and transparency.
